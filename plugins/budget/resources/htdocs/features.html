FEATURES OF OUR SERVICES

What your budget advisor can, and cannot do

The Hutt City budget and advocacy service offers free and confidential help to anyone in financial distress, including:

  • Debt collectors
  • Personal debt
  • Bill payments
  • No food
  • Hire Purchase payments
  • Benefit problems (WINZ)
  • IRD
  • Court Fines

  • We can act as your spokesperson to debt collection companies, including helping you to speak to them/negotiate with them only with a privacy waiver signed by you.
  • We can advise you on what help is available from WINZ and speak to them in your presence - an advisor is at WINZ offices in Lower Hutt and Naenae (and Wainuiomata by request) during the morning on the following days: Monday ; Tuesday ; Thursday.
  • We can help you apply for "last resort" options like Bankruptcy and Summary Instalment Orders
  • We are able to advise you about other help available including non-financial (eg. help with gambling, alcohol, mental health).
  • We can also put you in touch with doctors, mental health workers, or solicitors who may be able to help you.
  • We are free, confidential, impartial, non-discriminatory and fully trained.
