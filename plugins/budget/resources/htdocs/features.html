FEATURES OF OUR SERVICES

What your budget advisor can, and cannot do

The Hutt City budget and advocacy service offers free and confidential help to anyone in financial distress, including for:

  • Debt collectors
  • Personal debt
  • Bill payments
  • No food
  • Hire Purchase payments
  • Benefit problems (WINZ)
  • IRD
  • Court Fines

  • We can help you apply for "last resort" options like Bankruptcy and Summary Instalment Orders
  • We are able to advise you about other help available including non-financial (eg. help with gambling, alcohol, mental health).
  • We can also put you in touch with doctors, mental health workers, or solicitors who may be able to help you.
  • We are free, confidential, impartial, non-discriminatory and fully trained.
